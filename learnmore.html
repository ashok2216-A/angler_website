Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Angler and how it can transform your learning experience.

How accurate are the AI-generated notes?

Our AI system has been trained on thousands of educational videos and achieves over 95% accuracy in extracting key information. For specialized technical content, users can review and edit notes to ensure complete accuracy.

What types of videos work best with Angler?

Angler works with any educational content on YouTube, but performs exceptionally well with lectures, tutorials, educational explainers, and course videos. The more structured the content, the better the results.

How does the pricing model work?

Angler offers a freemium model. The free tier allows processing of videos up to 10 minutes in length with basic features. Premium tiers unlock longer videos, advanced quiz generation, PDF exports, and team collaboration features.

Can I use Angler for my classroom or institution?

Absolutely! We offer special education pricing for teachers, schools, and universities. Our institutional plans include additional features like student progress tracking, content libraries, and LMS integration.

Is my data secure?

We take data privacy seriously. All your notes and learning materials are securely stored with enterprise-grade encryption. We never share your data with third parties, and you retain full ownership of all content you create.
